Pascal's Principle

Key Formulas:
P₁ = P₂ - Pressure is transmitted equally
F₂ = F₁ × (A₁/A₂) - Force amplification
P = F/A - Pressure calculation

Pascal's principle states that pressure applied to a confined fluid is transmitted equally in all directions. This allows hydraulic systems to amplify force.

Piezoelectric Effect

The piezoelectric effect converts mechanical stress directly into electrical energy. When pressure is applied to certain crystals, they generate an electric charge proportional to the applied force.

Energy Formulas:
P = V²/R - Power calculation
E = 0.5 × C × V² - Energy stored in capacitor

Bridge Rectifier

A bridge rectifier converts alternating current (AC) to direct current (DC) using four diodes arranged in a diamond pattern. This is essential for converting the AC output from piezoelectric elements into usable DC power.

How it works:

  1. During positive half-cycle, current flows through diodes D1 and D2
  2. During negative half-cycle, current flows through diodes D3 and D4
  3. The output is always positive, creating pulsed DC
  4. A capacitor smooths the pulsed DC into steady DC

Energy Harvesting Concepts

Energy harvesting involves capturing ambient energy from the environment and converting it into usable electrical power. Common sources include:

  • Kinetic Energy: Motion, vibration, footsteps
  • Thermal Energy: Temperature differences
  • Solar Energy: Light from the sun
  • Radio Frequency: Electromagnetic waves

This project focuses on kinetic energy harvesting using human footsteps as the energy source.
